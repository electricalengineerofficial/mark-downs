**Examples:**
- "Ohm's Law Explained: Complete Guide for Engineers" ✅
- "Three-Phase Power: Industrial Applications & Calculations" ✅
- "VFD Motor Control: Setup, Wiring & Programming Guide" ✅

**NOT:** "Interesting Electrical Stuff" ❌

💬 Ask questions in the comments
